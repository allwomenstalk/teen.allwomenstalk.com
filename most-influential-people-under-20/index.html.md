# 30 Most Influential People under  20 ...

Whoever said our generation was lazy should really think again! Teens and young adults around the globe make the world a better place every day by inventing new technologies, inspiring other young people, developing charities, and much more!

## 1. Rowan Blanchard, 15

![hair, person, hairstyle, photography, beauty,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yy/ov/jdwh4y95582a0fbebf162953739550_600x600.jpg)

You might know her as the spunky star of Disney Channel’s “Girl Meets World" but since accruing more fame, she's put her popularity to good use by raising awareness about the importance of intersectional feminism. Well-known feminists such as Emma Watson have also shown Rowan support, and in 2015 she spoke at the UN’s Women Annual Summit. And get this-she was only 14!

## 2. Ahmed Mohomad, 15

![person, man, people, male, senior citizen,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l4/g0/fef52evn582a10285f036404270153_600x600.jpg)

Last year, this boy genius made news when school authorities assumed the home-made clock he had created was actually a bomb. The outrageous accusation sparked a national conversation about racial profiling in schools. Ahmed stayed strong throughout the process, and eventually got accepted into a prestigious school in Qatar.

## 3. Amandla Stenberg, 17

![hair, face, person, woman, black hair,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/a9/d3/lithbeg3582a10ab033ef465353991_480x480.jpg)

She may have lost in The Hunger Games, but Amandla is winning in life! Like Rowan, she uses her fame to raise awareness about an issue that’s important to her: appropriation of black culture.

## 4. Bindi Irwin, 17

![koala, mammal, vertebrate, nose, marsupial,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/m6/pf/lpzqpn6q582a10e79d100682775720_600x600.jpg)

Do you remember everyone’s favorite Crocodile Hunter, Steve Irwin? Well, at age 17, his daughter is following in his famous footsteps. While Bindi isn’t hunting any crocodiles, she is advocating for conservation efforts and last year she shined on Australia’s Dancing With the Stars.

## 5. Olivia Hallisley, 18

![hair, face, person, nose, blond,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xm/z4/ijpv2tw3582a12d877ae0538251018_486x486.jpg)

This brilliant teen is helping make the world healthy again. She discovered a treatment for Ebola that doesn’t require refrigeration-much more effective for victims of the virus in rural Africa. Her accomplishments didn’t go unnoticed; Olivia was recognized in the 2015 Google Science Fair.

## 6. Katie Ledecky, 19

![sports,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kb/fy/anwhn072582a146a53c84980062756_600x450.jpg)

With the olympics behind her, this female Michael Phelps is Stanford-bound! Beauty, talent, and brains!

## 7. Zendaya, 20

![eyebrow, hair, face, cheek, nose,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qr/xc/n47g419o582a15d551dc3493965562_600x600.jpg)

Due to her time with Disney, Zendaya has become a household name. She’s an icon of beauty and fashion, and the epitome of grace and charm. She even has a Barbie designed after her!

## 8. Flynn McGarry, 17

![person, cook, professional, chef, profession,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/au/z8/y3qwv1f8582c29678a5e2261786318_429x429.jpg)

This young man turned something we do every day, eat, into an absolute art form. His goal is to own the best restaurant in the world, and I’d say, after opening a world-renowned supper club already, Flynn is well on his way to achieving his goal!

## 9. Laurie Hernandez, 16

![person, face, eyebrow, hairstyle, head,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zl/ml/zeha3cpu582c2a0b88085262878826_600x600.jpg)

The Olympics brings many young athletes into the spotlight. Laurie Hernandez is one of them. Thanks to her, the U.S. team dominated gymnastics this year!

## 10. Simone Biles, 19

![clothing, swimwear, muscle, arm, leg,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ek/w7/y2onl31s582c2ad6d57ca951809185_441x441.jpg)

Laurie’s teammate was America’s sweetheart over summer at the Rio Olympics!

## 11. Sydney McLaughlin, 17

![human action, person, sports, athletics, endurance sports,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p2/p9/ll85wx2d582df8d09ea53873698750_480x480.jpg)

You might remember rooting for Sydney in the 2016 Rio Olympics, too. She represented the U.S. women’s track team this year and will hopefully run in Tokyo in 2020!

## 12. Chloe Kim, 16

![blue, footwear, sports, winter sport, ice skating,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k3/pl/d95xgutg582df921d02f8715273777_600x600.jpg)

Look for Chloe Kim at the 2018 Winter Olympics! In South Korea, Kim will probably add a few medals to her current collection of 3 gold X-games medals.

## 13. Cassandra Lin, 18

![facial expression, person, girl, beauty, smile,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/r3/yp/x53btzn2582df968d1667708815353_600x600.jpg)

You’ve heard of TGIF, right? No, not Thank God it’s Friday, even though that’s true, too. I mean Cassandra Lin’s project “Turn Grease Into Fuel” which recycles grease to use it as biodiesel that helps heat family’s homes.

## 14. Hailee Steinfeld, 19

![hair, person, black hair, image, girl,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yy/v1/tstjykh5582df9e4706dc584842854_600x600.jpg)

You probably saw her for the first time in Pitch Perfect 2, where Hailee debuted her musical skills to a wider audience than ever before in her career. She’s still on the rise, and we can look forward to her continuing to influence the music industry!

## 15. Maddie Ziegler, 14

![hair, person, photography, beauty, girl,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sf/aa/moadlhfy582dfa3dee2f6271496933_598x595.jpg)

Maddie is a 14 year old dancer who just started her own clothing line in 2016!

## 16. Skai Jackson, 14

![hair, face, eyebrow, nose, cheek,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dj/e9/hzzfouxu582dfac6d3ec9644848704_600x598.jpg)

If you don't know her yet for her acting career, you definitely know this sassy and gorgeous 14 year old from the memes! She also speaks out about cyberbullying and advocates for staying true to yourself.

## 17. Gaten Matarazzo, 14

![hair, face, person, nose, hairstyle,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ql/cp/rfrm4i4v582dfb09b7bdf344193798_450x450.jpg)

![clothing, coat, outerwear, trench coat, fashion,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/td/xg/gh08p6ky582e0867d494b589660150_600x600.jpg)

## 19. Yara Shahidi, 16

![VOGUE, Teen Vogue, hair, color, face,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sr/qr/w6foptnj582e08c29025d124212950_600x600.jpg)

![face, eyebrow, hair, clothing, nose,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ya/rb/asy45irp582e098decf32743984363_600x600.jpg)

![hair, clothing, hairstyle, fashion, sleeve,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jx/u7/a5detx40582e0af48c60f747492121_428x428.jpg)

## 22. Maisie Williams, 19

![hair, face, eyebrow, clothing, nose,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o4/u2/n7rjfw2v582e0b0ed54a0377621157_564x564.jpg)

## 23. Camila Cabello, 19

![hair, face, black hair, hairstyle, beauty,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ua/bf/m9walgx8582e0b3ab8af5583580244_600x600.jpg)

## 24. Barbie Ferreira, 19

![hair, eyebrow, face, person, nose,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/p0/sg/qpiebcbk582e0b5f757d9640135797_600x600.jpg)

Despite her name, Barbie is working against the unrealistic, barbie-like demands for young women produced by the media. She's a successful model who demonstrates confidence in all of her “fiercely real” photos.

![hair, person, male, man, hairstyle,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kw/g1/ljrkmpth582e0b7c571c5811863869_500x500.jpg)

## 26. Jaden Smith, 18

![hair, hairstyle, beauty, head, fashion,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/m0/ji/d2tke5xq582e0b95a103c352952231_600x600.jpg)

![black hair, face, hair, eyebrow, black,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cq/wa/m03o5jcb582e0badd8bb9394135894_451x567.jpg)

There are so many inspiring young people in our generation! Which ones do you look up to?
