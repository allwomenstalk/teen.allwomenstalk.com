# 7 Trips to Embark on with Your Friends ...

If this is your last year of high school and you have been saving up on trips to take with friends during the span of four years, it is time to narrow down your list. Personally, I think that everyone should take a small trip with friends before everyone heads off to college. It is a great way to say your final goodbyes and do something memorable. After all, soon your life will change completely! So here are some ideas for trips to take with friends the summer before college.

## 1. California

![California](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/10195154_8938_600x450.jpg)

Los Angeles, California is one of the best trips to take with friends. The weather is always nice and warm, and there is no limit on what you can do. You can hit the beach almost everyday and just enjoy the California vibe. Just think through your expenses beforehand and you are bound to have a fun filled vacation! Not to mention there is also Disneyland, the happiest place on earth!

## 2. Cancun

![Cancun](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/10195213_9880_600x400.jpg)

If you really want to get fancy, Cancun is known to be one of the best places to visit with friends. While most people visit it during their college years, there is nobody stopping you from checking the place out early on. Cancun has amazing beaches and tourist resorts that will only add on to your already memorable summer.

## 3. London

![London](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/10195240_7544_600x359.jpg)

I think London is on almost everyone’s list of places to visit. It has its own charm with British accents, beautiful sights and attractions. It is a city that has a lot to offer, so why not explore it with a group of friends while you are young and free of responsibilities?

## 4. Paris

![Paris](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/10195306_6586_600x450.jpg)

What girl hasn’t dreamed of visiting the city of love, eating macarons and shopping in the beautiful stores of Paris? I think it is almost mandatory for each person in the world to get the chance to see the Eiffel Tower and the Louvre museum. Getting away from your homeland to Paris would be the greatest vacation after high school.

## 5. Maui

![Maui](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/10195359_3945_600x440.jpg)

If you have always wanted to visit Hawaii, Maui would be a great place to consider. It has beautiful beaches with secluded areas in addition to busy restaurants and attraction places. It is truly a beautiful place to visit, especially when you have your friends to keep you company.

## 6. Santorini

![Santorini](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/10195508_0359_600x391.jpg)

Ever since I watched 'The Sisterhood of the Traveling Pants,’ Greece became one of the places I had to visit. Santorini is known for its amazing views, stunning sunsets and clear beaches. It is a picture perfect place where everything is just brighter and better!

## 7. New York

![New York](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2014/01/10195525_6582_600x400.jpg)

New York is a city that never sleeps! From personal experience of living here for a number of years, I can truly say that you can never run out of things to do. New York offers a variety of cultures, cuisines and types of entertainment. Between Broadway shows, Time Square and outstanding restaurants, you won’t find the time to be bored in a city like this!

So if you are ready to take the trip of your lifetime, it is time to get planning. An experience like this requires a lot of budget counting and date planning! Which place are you set on exploring with your closest group of friends first?
