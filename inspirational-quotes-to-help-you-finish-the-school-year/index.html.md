# 7 Inspirational Quotes to Help You Finish the School Year ...

As the school year draws to a close, it can be so easy to lose sight of your goal, and in those times, you need inspirational quotes to help you finish the school year. You’re looking forward to summer, sunshine, and no homework, and school is not part of the equation. If you’re trying to make it through the school year, try to think of these quotes to help you finish the school year the next time you’re stuck in a rut. We all know that being in a rut while at school is no fun at all, but these quotes should absolutely help you! Remember, school is almost over, hang in there!

## 1. Nelson Mandela

![Nelson Mandela](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wa/g5/uvx72ws1_600x422.jpg)

When time is closing in, sometimes you really need quotes to help you finish the school year - like this one. An education truly can help you change the world, even if it doesn’t always seem like it when you’re studying for your English final exam. While school might not seem all that important when you are going through it, it will absolutely help you in the long run!

## 2. Louis Pasteur

![Louis Pasteur](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hl/ap/jq4udznu_600x520.jpg)

Sometimes you just need to remind yourself to be tenacious, especially as the school year draws to a close. You may just want to quit everything and shut down, but you can’t just yet. You’re so close to finishing! Be tenacious!

## 3. Aristotle

![Aristotle](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dc/pr/gf8h0597_600x397.jpg)

This can definitely come in handy as papers are due, projects need to be presented, and finals are coming up. You may think school is horrible right now, but eventually it will be so worth it. You’ll work it all out!

## 4. Anthony J. D’Angelo

![Anthony J. D’Angelo](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z7/gd/a3m0ltet_600x600.jpg)

It can be hard to want to learn all of the time. It can be a lot of hard work, and there can be moments when you just don’t necessarily have the energy. Rather than let yourself get discouraged, try to think of it as a passion, like this quote. If you have a fiery passion for what you’re learning, it won’t be nearly as difficult to actually learn! Remember, passion is something that most people have, but don't often channel it to whatever they are trying to do. Channel your passion!

## 5. Benjamin Mays

![Benjamin Mays](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/aw/0r/e453kabp_600x471.jpg)

If you actually have a goal you’re attempting to reach, you’re already miles ahead of a lot of people. Set some goals, and work to complete them. Even if you don’t complete them, at least you had goals to work toward!

## 6. Epictetus

![Epictetus](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fj/gp/dde3g0yr_600x600.jpg)

This quote always inspires me when I’m in a rut in school. You can’t learn what you already know. It’s that simple. If you’re not learning anything new, you’re not learning. Isn't that the truth? I love this quote!

## 7. Albert Einstein

![Albert Einstein](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/x3/v5/ct1k31x3_600x400.jpg)

This quote is another that can inspire anyone in a learning rut. It can be easy to simply memorize the information you need to know, especially when you’re in a finals rush. Try to understand the material instead. It will help you in the long run, and when you’re tested on the material.

What quotes do you think of when you’re looking for inspiration in school? These are just a few of my favorites, but I’d love to read a few of yours! Let me know your favorites in the comments!
