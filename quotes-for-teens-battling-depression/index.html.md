# 7 Quotes for Teens Battling Depression ...

There are numerous quotes for teens battling depression, as depression in youths is an utter tragedy. People tell you that these are "the best years of your life," but some days, you can't even be motivated enough to get out of bed and face the world. When you discover how truly strange the world is during adolescence, it's easy to slip into a depression. Hopefully these quotes for teens battling depression will help you pick yourself up.

## 1. J.K. Rowling

![Smart Turnout,text,font,organ,brand,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qa/mw/fc54l_smart-turnout_text_font_organ_brand_495x565.jpg)

First, it's important to define what depression is. Author J.K. Rowling poignantly describes what it's like to feel depressed in this quote for teens battling depression. As a teenager, it's hard to understand anything at all, especially something as dark and unfamiliar as depression. And when you are depressed, it's nearly impossible to imagine ever existing outside of that dark, unfamiliar state.

## 2. Henry Wadsworth Longfellow

![text,handwriting,font,calligraphy,writing,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k3/c3/ax1if_text_handwriting_font_calligraphy_writing_600x600.jpg)

A key to finding comfort in any situation is recognizing that you are not alone. Everyone you encounter has secret battles they have fought or are currently fighting. You don't know it when they hand you your Starbucks cup, or when they hold the door open for you, but they know what it's like to be broken just like you do. It's tragic, but reassuring to know that you are never alone.

## 3. Dodie Smith

![text,font,purple,logo,line,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/le/eb/jy0rk_text_font_purple_logo_line_600x600.jpg)

Generally, there is no "cure" for depression, but that doesn't mean you shouldn't try to make yourself feel better. Unfortunately, some people try to cure their suffering with self-harm and eating disorders, but these actions will only cause you to spiral further into your depression. Do noble deeds and take hot baths instead, or anything along those lines. Healthy, positive actions that take your mind off of your depression can never hurt!

## 4. John Keats

![text,handwriting,font,blackboard,writing,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ls/i8/c55cx_text_handwriting_font_blackboard_writing_600x489.jpg)

As a teenager, it's difficult to see that a future lies ahead of you, especially if you're battling depression. But in the future, you will be a different person, and the person you become will be a result of your depression. You will mature into a complete human being, with a unique soul.

## 5. Shayamalan

![Dress Shop,Yoopies,text,font,handwriting,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/c2/c6/nd4y7_dress-shop_yoopies_text_font_handwriting_403x275.jpg)

This is another truth that's hard to accept when you're young. How could depression-which reduced you to a broken, empty person-make you stronger? Well, the answer lies in the question, because you are not broken and empty, you just think you are. Actually, this stage of your life is a period during which your spirit is being built up and fortified so that you can boldly face obstacles in your future.

## 6. David Mitchell

![text,font,handwriting,brand,writing,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rl/nl/c0xiu_text_font_handwriting_brand_writing_403x275.jpg)

Depression is a monster that makes you feel like a mouse. But you are not small, meek, and helpless. Compared to your friends, in your own mind you may appear defective, but to everyone else who knows that you wake up every day even though you really don't want to, you are superwoman. Your resilience is inspiring others every day that you keep going.

## 7. Paulo Coelho

![EFG Bank,font,product,biology,organ,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pv/i3/w55yw_efg-bank_font_product_biology_organ_402x265.jpg)

Depression is like a sword that strikes you every day, but never kills you. Sometimes, you may be tempted to take up the sword yourself, and end it all right now. But if you endure, in the end, you'll emerge from your battle with scars from head to toe. Depression, the sword that caused these gruesome emotional scars, won't be praised nor recognized. Rather, your scars will tell a story of resilience and strength that not everyone can boast about.

Depression is a terribly scary feeling of hopelessness and loss. However, it's not the only feeling you will have during your teenage years. If you take the advice these quotes have to offer to heart, you're one step closer to defeating depression. Which of these quotes meant the most to you?
