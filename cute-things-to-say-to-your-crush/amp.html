Looking for cute things to say to your crush? No problem! You must have your eyes on someone, and you find yourself wondering what to say to them. If you're not sure how to reach out, this list of 73 Cute Things To Say To Your Crush might be able to help! Being cute is the key here. Quotes about saying “I love you” won't always get a cute response, but a nice compliment might be just the ticket to a new relationship.


Confidence is key here, even if you're feeling anxious. There's nothing wrong about saying cute things to your crush, and showing that you're both sweet and sincere will get results. Keep in mind, though, that these aren't the only cute things you can say to your crush! These are just a sample of some cute things to say. You can use this list as a starting point, but don't feel locked into anything.


Everyone has their own style, and you should feel free to adjust any of these cute phrases to fit your personality. Just be sure that you're being genuine. If it feels fake or phony, then it probably won't get the results you want.


So, without further ado, 73 Cute Things to Say to Your Crush!!



1. Cute Things to Say to Your Crush after a Date:


❤️ Sometimes you'll find the person you're looking for right in front of you.


This does sound a little like a line, but it's adorable. Let them know you're not just looking for a casual date; they could be the one.


❤️ I appreciate you being... you.


This is an excellent method to express gratitude for your crush's unique personality. You can see that they are sincere and honest, which you admire.


❤️ You are playing a big part in my plans for the future.


❤️ Are you aware of how cute you are?


If they're fussing with their hair, glancing in the mirror, or even asking you how their attire looks, this is the best chance to say something.


❤️ “I could stare at you all day!”

This is particularly cute and effective at the very end of the date - right before the good night kiss!


❤️ How did you become so great? 


This not only lets them know you have a crush on them, but it also demonstrates your want to learn more about them.

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I say to get my crush's attention?
Just be yourself! A cute compliment or mentioning something you both like can work wonders. Like, 'I love your taste in music!'
How do I compliment my crush without sounding awkward?
Keep it genuine and simple. Instead of overthinking, try saying something like, 'Your laugh is contagious!'
Is it okay to be cheesy when talking to my crush?
Totally! A little bit of cheese can be super sweet. Think something like, 'I think you’re the reason my favorite songs make me smile.'
What are some adorable things to say to make my crush happy?
Go for sincere sweet words. Tell them, 'You always know how to brighten my day!' or 'Talking to you is the best part of my day.'
Can compliments actually help me start a conversation with my crush?
Definitely! A sincere compliment like, 'I love how passionate you are about your hobbies,' can open up a fun and engaging chat.
