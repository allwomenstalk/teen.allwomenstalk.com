# 17 Fun  Challenges to do with Friends when Youre All Bored ...

**Stuck in a Rut? Tired of the Same Old Hangouts? Discover These Fun Challenges to Do with Friends!**

Ever had one of those days when you're sitting with your besties, scrolling endlessly through social media, and yet everything feels like a rerun of a bad sitcom? **Fun challenges** are the perfect antidote to break the monotony and spice up your get-togethers. Trust me, as someone who's experienced the trials and tribulations of boredom more times than I care to admit, I can assure you these activities are life-savers. Plus, we're in 2024, folks! Let's leave the uneventful hangs back in the past.

One day, my friends and I were all sitting around the living room, heads buried in our phones, when someone jokingly suggested a "try-not-to-laugh challenge." Before long, our boring afternoon had turned into a hysterical memory we still laugh about to this day. Sometimes, all you need is a good list of [17 fun challenges](#1) to turn a dull moment into a laughter-filled spectacle. And oh boy, do I have a list for you!

These challenges cater to a range of interests, from those with a knack for creativity to others who just love a good dare. Whether you're feeling a bit inventive or downright daring, there's something for everyone. Ever tried a _hot pepper challenge_? It's as fiery as it sounds, but also as memorable. What about testing your culinary skills with a _mystery ingredient cook-off_? If you think you know the difference between paprika and cinnamon, think again!

Are you an adrenaline junkie? Some challenges are not just fun; they’re downright exhilarating. Take the 'Supermarket Sweep' challenge, where teams race against the clock to grab every item on a wild grocery list. Imagine the looks you'll get as you dash through the aisles! The laughs alone are worth the effort.

For those who are a little more subdued, don’t worry. We’ve got challenges that involve less physical exertion but are just as engaging. How about a _book reading marathon_? Or perhaps a couple of rounds of [trivia night at home](#14) to get those brain cells pumping. Plus, it doesn’t hurt to sprinkle in a bit of competition to see who reigns supreme as the trivia champion.

Now, picture this: it’s late, you’ve exhausted all the video streaming options, and everyone is dying for some excitement. Ever considered a _midnight scavenger hunt_? It’s as thrilling as it sounds and puts a mystical twist on the whole experience. Or, if you're in the mood for something truly unique, the _"try not to sing" challenge_ will have you mustering every ounce of willpower not to belt out that catchy tune.

By the end of these escapades, you and your pals will have more inside jokes than you know what to do with. So why spend another night aimlessly scrolling through your phones? Dive into these adventures and make memories that you'll be talking about well into 2025. Let’s get started, shall we?

## 1. Oreo Challenge

This involves pulling apart an Oreo, sticking the frosted side to your forehead, and having a race against friends to try to work the cookie all the way down to your mouth. A fun race that finishes with a tasty treat! This is one of my favorite **challenges to do with friends**.

## 2. Guess the Song Challenge

We all think we are Whitney Houston deep down inside, so showcase your talents to your friends by humming or singing a part of a song, giving a point to the person who guesses correctly first!

## 3. Try Not to Laugh Challenge

Isn’t it always the way that you want to laugh the most in situations when you can’t? This kind of game showcases that perfectly!

## 4. Cracker Challenge

Try to eat and get down six Saltines or cream crackers (if you’re a Brit) in 60 seconds. Sounds easy, right? Wrong! You won’t know until you have tried for yourself!

## 5. Chubby Bunny Challenge

Try your own version of this YouTube classic where you and a friend take it in turns to see how many marshmallows you can get in your mouth whilst still being able to say “chubby bunny”!

## 6. Watermelon Challenge

The challenge is to try to eat a watermelon in two minutes. Not so bad, right? Well, add to the equation the fact that you aren’t allowed to use your hands!

## 7. The Lemon Challenge

This one involves trying to eat more lemon slices that your friends in one minute. Be sure to capture all of the hilarious sour faces that get created!

## 8. Powdered Donut Challenge

You have to eat five powdered donuts in a timeframe without being allowed anything to drink or to wipe your mouth and hands!

## 9. Pop Rocks Challenge

Test how many pop rocks you can hold in your mouth at one time. Even though it feels like your head might explode, we promise it won’t!

## 10. Pringles Taste Test

Buy a bunch of different flavoured Pringles and have your friends try to guess which varieties you are giving them.

## 11. Bubblegum Challenge

How many pieces of bubblegum can you fit in your mouth? Who can blow the biggest bubble?

## 12. Mentos and Coke Challenge

This is another internet classic, just make sure that you are outdoors to see what happens when you throw a load of Mentos into a bottle of Coke! Or even better, make the mixture in your mouth!

## 13. Baked Beans and Chopsticks Challenge

Put a minute on the clock and see how many baked beans you can pick up and eat using a pair of trusty chopsticks!

## 14. No Mirror Hairstyle Challenge

Without the use of mirrors, see who can create the best looking hairstyle just using the power of their hands and imagination alone.

## 15. Blindfolded Makeup Challenge

Blindfold a friend and get them to apply your makeup as carefully as they can. Do the same to them and then ask another friend to judge who did it best!

## 16. Blindfolded Self Portrait Challenge

Find out what your own mind’s eye really thinks of you by trying to paint a self portrait whilst blindfolded. It’s sure to bring hilarious results!

## 17. Accent Challenge

Put a bunch of different accents on pieces of paper in a hat, and take turns picking one out and doing your best to get your friends to guess correctly. Don’t be embarrassed; we’re all terrible at this one!
