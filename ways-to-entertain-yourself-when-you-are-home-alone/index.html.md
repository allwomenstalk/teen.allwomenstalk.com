# 7 Ways to Entertain Yourself when You Are Home Alone ...

If you live in a big family you don’t always have the whole house to yourself but when you do, it is hard to find ways to entertain yourself. It feels very out of the ordinary but this is not something you should simply let go. This is your chance to enjoy some much needed alone time in your own home. Here are 7 ways to entertain yourself when you are home all alone.

## 1. Full on Karaoke Session

The first thing that comes to mind when you are home alone is having a full on karaoke session. There is nobody to bother with loud music and singing, so don’t be afraid to turn up the speakers and put on your own show. Nothing feels better than singing on the top of your lungs to your favorite tunes and not being judged by others! So if you are looking for ways to entertain yourself, turn to your playlist!

## 2. Have a Long Movie Marathon

Sometimes watching movies alone is more relaxing than you would think. So set up yourself in comfort, grab a blanket, get some popcorn and enjoy a couple of flicks. You won’t have your kids or siblings running around and your parents asking you to do things; you will finally have the peaceful and entertaining marathon session you have always wanted. You won’t even notice how fast the time will pass!

## 3. Experiment in the Kitchen

At one point or another you will probably get hungry, so what better way to spend time than by cooking yourself a nice meal? You can always experiment with ingredients and pull up recipes from online. It can be an interesting experience and if everything fails, nobody has to know!

## 4. Video Chat with Friends

If you are in a need of contact with people, you can always have a video chat party with friends. You can catch up with them and gossip (admit it, we all do it) without worrying that someone will overhear. It is the same thing as talking on the phone but better!

## 5. Catch up on Shows

When you run out of things to talk about and movies to watch, you can always catch up on your favorite tv shows. You can spend hours in front of the TV or on the computer without worrying about your household chores and enjoy some pure relaxation time. We all need it sooner or later!

## 6. Do Some Yoga Poses

Exercising at home in front of your family can get a little weird, so this is your chance to get your body moving without having the trouble of commuting to the gym. The peacefulness of the house would make the perfect environment for some yoga. You can relax your muscles and find your inner peace.

## 7. Invite a Friend over

Of course when you get a little lonely in your own company, you can always invite a few friends. Friends can always find things to do and topics to talk about. So you can be confident that you will spend your you time without boring yourself to death!

Staying at home by yourself can feel a little atypical especially if this is your first time in years. However, use it as an opportunity to take some time for yourself and enjoy some time away from people you see every day. It can be healthy and cleansing. What are some of your favorite things to do when you are home alone?
