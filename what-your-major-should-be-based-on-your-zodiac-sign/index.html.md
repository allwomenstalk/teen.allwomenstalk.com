# What Your Major Should Be Based on Your Zodiac Sign ...

Want to know **what your major should be based on your zodiac sign**? If you are the kind of student who has lots of different academic interests and are proficient in a lot of different areas, then trying to work out what your college major should be can be a difficult and time-consuming decision to make. Whilst some people might have a clear career path or future dream laid out ahead of them, you might be much more open to persuasion when it comes to choosing what your main subject of study should be. If you have any faith at all in the world of astrology, then the month that you were born in might be able to reveal some answers! Here is **what your major should be based on your zodiac sign**.

## 1. Aries

You are the perfect combination of active and eager to help others, so a major that has lots to do with physical fitness and sports science would be perfect. You can pave the way to becoming a phys ed teacher.

## 2. Taurus

You are the kind of person who always does well with numbers, whether financial or hypothetical! You should put your talents to good use in the form of a math major.

## 3. Gemini

You are one of the best communicators in both your family and your friendship group, so there is no other direction for you to go than toward a fitting communications major!

## 4. Cancer

You have a great desire to nurture, and this type of talent would be put to best use in the form of an education major where you can go on to spread your enthusiasm and talents.

## 5. Leo

You are a natural born actor, life and soul of the party! There is no other route to go down than that of a drama degree!

## 6. Virgo

You are the kind of person who will be successful in pretty much anything that you pick, so why not take advantage of that and shoot for the stars with a sparkling law degree?

## 7. Libra

You are definitely the most talented and creative person you know, so there is really no other choice for you than an arts degree.

## 8. Scorpio

You have the special ability to be able to think on your feet and make split decisions really quickly, and these types of skills are essential for a medical major.

## 9. Sagittarius

You are a natural gatherer of information and you have an innate understanding of people, which makes you the perfect candidate to be a sociology major.

## 10. Capricorn

Your best skills are the areas of following specific directions and working extra hard, and this work ethic can be put to best use in the area of science.

## 11. Aquarius

You are at your best when you are being entertained whilst learning at the same time, and this kind of stimulation can always be found in the area of philosophy.

## 12. Pisces

Any true Pisces counts food as their greatest love, so if you want to live up to your zodiac sign, then a future in the culinary field is definitely your calling!
