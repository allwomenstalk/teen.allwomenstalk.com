# 7 Smart Strategies for Crushing Your Academic Goals This Year

If, at the beginning of your school year, you set some pretty ambitious objectives and now you fear that you might not be able to fulfill them, just read on and discover a few very helpful tips on how to accomplish a school goal. Fulfilling your dreams and achieving all your goals is not always easy, since there are a lot of things that can interfere with your plans. Sometimes it’s pretty easy to fall back into those bad habits, such as procrastination, poor research or cramming, and you might find yourself far away from those initial plans you made. In those situations, you will need to work a little bit harder to stick to your plans and achieve success in your school career. Here are a few very efficient things you can do if you want to learn how to accomplish a school goal:

## 1. Set Realistic Goals

One of the most important things you should do if you want to learn how to accomplish a school goal is to set some realistic, achievable and specific goals. Make sure you know what you want to achieve, how you plan on doing it and that you have all the tools you need to succeed. If your goal seems too big or too difficult, try breaking it down into smaller parts, so you can work and focus on one thing at a time. This approach is highly recommended by experts at [Psychology Today](https://www.psychologytoday.com/us/basics/goal-setting) to help maintain focus.

## 2. Write down Your Goal

The next step you should take to reach your objective is to simply write down your goal. Once you put it on paper, you will take it more seriously and you will permanently be reminded of your dream. You could try writing it down in a journal or on a sticky note that you can place near your bedside or somewhere you will be able to see it very often, since this will motivate you to stay on track to reach your goal.

## 3. Break Your Goal down

As I’ve said before, if your goal seems too big and it’s starting to scare you, you could try breaking it down into smaller pieces, so you can work on one thing at a time. This way, once you see that you can do it and that it’s not actually as impossible as you first thought it would be, you will be more determined to do everything in your power to reach your objective. Also, you will be able to celebrate many small victories instead of just a big one and this will give you even more reasons to feel happy and proud of yourself.

## 4. Avoid Distractions

When you’re working towards achieving your goal, make sure you avoid all distractions, so that nothing will interfere with your plans. I’m not saying you shouldn’t watch TV or use the Internet, just make sure that during that time you dedicate to accomplishing your goal, you avoid all those things that might cause you problems. Researching [how to stay focused while studying](https://allwomenstalk.com/how-to-stay-focused-while-studying/) can also give you additional strategies to stay on track.

## 5. Stay Positive

Even if things don’t work out the way you planned sometimes, try to stay positive and don’t let anything get you down. If your goal is important to you, then don’t let anything stand in your way. Don’t be so hard on yourself and don’t let a small setback break your motivation! Work hard, keep your goal in mind and don’t give up!

## 6. Reward Yourself

Every time you manage to accomplish a small part of your goal, make sure you reward yourself; after all, you deserve it. This will motivate you to keep going and work hard to fulfill all your dreams and reach your objectives. For example, every once in a while, when you complete a goal, treat yourself to an ice cream or go buy that book you always wanted to read. Taking breaks is essential, and you can find more [self-care tips for students](https://allwomenstalk.com/self-care-tips-for-students/) on our site to keep your energy up.

## 7. Make It Happen

Even if this sounds easier said than done, you shouldn’t worry, because if you work hard and if you have enough patience, there’s nothing you can’t do. Make a plan and stick to it no matter what. Even if sometimes, you might not feel very motivated or willing to work, you shouldn’t be alarmed, because everyone faces times like these when their motivation is a bit low. Just try to keep your goal in mind, do everything you can to fulfill it and don’t give up!
