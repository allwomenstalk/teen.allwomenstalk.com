# Try These 17 Pretty Trends for Prom 2016 ...

Prom will be here before you know it, and if you haven't chosen your prom dress yet, it's time! Here are a few trends to be aware of while you shop... they're all so gorgeous!

## 1. Pastels

![Pastels](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ab/jh/pj4y0q7c56f9509dc7e10677627411_600x850.jpg)

Red is hot right now, but since everyone else will be wearing it, why not opt for a pretty pastel in pink, blue, or green?

## 2. Split-Skirt

![Split-Skirt](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oz/wl/vq2oljsf56f9509e709bf250326151_600x870.jpg)

The split-skirt trend is just gorgeous! I've seen it like this, where both skirts are long, or where the inside skirt is shorter than the outside skirt.

## 3. High-Low Hemlines

![High-Low Hemlines](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/th/ic/eclmlbnb56f9509f14a1b863411476_600x917.jpg)

How flirty and fun! High-low hemlines are unique, and they're so cute!

## 4. Lace

![Lace](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/di/mu/o8zpyf5z56f9509fbb21f904376522_600x898.jpg)

Lace is making a big comeback in prom dresses this year, but you don't have to wear it all over - the lace crop top could really work!

## 5. Vintage Styling

![Vintage Styling](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/t5/ah/ckpznkms56f950a07ab46134259017_600x813.jpg)

Prom dresses this season have definitely been inspired by Downton Abbey, so you might want to consider a vintage-inspired dress.

## 6. Mermaid Dresses

![Mermaid Dresses](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sj/py/y4xyyz3w56f950a16804e800367154_600x900.jpg)

Who doesn't want to be a mermaid? The silhouette is so pretty, and if you find one in the right color, it'll complete the look.

## 7. Cut-outs

![Cut-outs](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jn/q7/puhbsnw456f950a25ef72926668340_600x931.jpg)

Some dress codes for prom have forbidden cutouts, but if your code is okay with them, you should consider cutouts! They're very unique.

## 8. Off-the-Shoulder

![Off-the-Shoulder](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wz/kb/ekia1kgl56f950a38c323316074533_600x840.jpg)

Talk about drama! Any prom dress with an off-the-shoulder top would really make a statement!

## 9. Sheer Skirts

![Sheer Skirts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j7/ur/j29qhahr56f950a477300716798856_600x859.jpg)

Sheer skirts, either just one layer of sheer at the bottom of the skirt, or multiple sheer layers all over (like this one), are really on trend this prom season!

## 10. Side Slits

![Side Slits](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bh/lo/msxs2ski56f950a584a66662117741_600x852.jpg)

Side slit don't have to be too sexy; they can just be fun! They make it easier to dance, which might be why they're making a comeback.

## 11. Crop Tops

![Crop Tops](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ko/lb/njrdknm356f950a69f4b9332259048_600x939.jpg)

Crop tops are so on trend everywhere else, of course they're popular for prom, too!

## 12. Florals

![Florals](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jm/an/iri23ty256f950a738ca2140392730_415x600.jpg)

Fab, feminine florals are back, and they're so much fun!

## 13. Backless Dresses

![Backless Dresses](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/zy/dl/c14y3ck156f950a88fcc6660026342_600x910.jpg)

Demure in the front, but showing a little skin in back - that's the allure of backless prom dresses, like this one!

## 14. Sequins

![Sequins](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u5/c6/ilhqc13456f950a95e4b9749032667_600x932.jpg)

Sequin are all about sparkle and shine! Find a dress with simple lines and the sequins can turn it from drab to fab!

## 15. Strapless

![Strapless](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/s2/zp/cgkne0jw56f950a9dcc23531962623_600x931.jpg)

If you can pull it off, why not try the strapless trend? The right bra will make all the difference, but if you can find one that works, this might be a fun trend to try!

## 16. Gorgeous Embroidery

![Gorgeous Embroidery](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/a2/sg/j9awhjo056f950aa12338077620734_600x831.jpg)

This dress combines several 2016 prom trends: the crop top, the sheer panel, and gorgeous boho-inspired embroidery.

## 17. Classic Ballgowns

![Classic Ballgowns](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ge/cg/iad4pcnb56f950aa129b6687551244_600x795.jpg)

You can never go wrong with a classic ballgown, especially one with modern updates - like the neckline on this one.
