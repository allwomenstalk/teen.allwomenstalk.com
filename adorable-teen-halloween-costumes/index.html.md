# Cheshire Cat Costume and Other Youth Friendly Ideas to Make Your Halloween a Blast ...

Finding Halloween costumes can be a huge struggle for some teens and young adults! You've outgrown many costumes, but you're not yet ready for revealing adult costumes. I’ve got the perfect solution: this article filled with teen friendly costumes from a **Cheshire cat costume** to the Morton salt girl. You won’t be able to resist these costumes this year or years to come!

## 1. Cheshire Cat

![Cheshire Cat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ch/w8/k3u5eece_475x641.jpg)

As a child, the Cheshire Cat from Alice in Wonderland always intrigued me! If you were also fascinated by this pink and purple cat, this may be the costume for you!

## 2. Gumball Machine

![Gumball Machine](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jt/jo/e5u3z0o9_401x600.jpg)

For crafty teens, you can make your own Halloween costume! A positively adorable idea is this gumball machine dress.

## 3. The 80's

![The 80's](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qx/v6/mn05zvrl_400x484.jpg)

The 1980's is a great decade to play up and make extremely dramatic! As an added bonus, this outfit is one you can easily create all on your own!

## 4. Human Slurpee

![Human Slurpee](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z8/v1/ofqidedw_510x640.jpg)

A silly, fun costume option is one like this-a giant Slurpee!

## 5. M&M

![M&M](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/u1/p8/h768qin8_528x800.jpg)

If you're a true fan of these candy-coated chocolates, you should consider dressing up as a frilly M&M this Halloween!

## 6. Steampunk

![Steampunk](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ey/k1/sfs99sob_340x453.jpg)

For some teens, this steampunk costume is a little bit mature, and I wouldn't recommend wearing it to school. But it can always be made more modest with some tights or leggings!

## 7. Nerd

![Nerd](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ld/fn/lok933jn_400x408.jpg)

Another costume you can easily assemble at home is a nerdy costume! This one focuses on Hello Kitty paraphernalia, but you can incorporate any of your nerdy obsessions!

## 8. Mad Hatter

![Mad Hatter](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/at/vj/v6d8g35c_250x312.jpg)

Like like the **Cheshire Cat costume** from before, Alice in Wonderland fans will love this chic, revamped Mad Hatter costume!

## 9. Cake

![Cake](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oi/dj/akzogivw_360x513.jpg)

Celebrate your favorite treat with this quirky costume!

## 10. Glamorous Goddess

![Glamorous Goddess](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n0/h4/df4naipt_340x448.jpg)

[.](https://goo.gl/iZ4NgV)
Get all glitzy and glamorous in this golden Greek Goddess costume!

## 11. Cookie Monster

![Cookie Monster](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pz/zg/twcz9inp_300x300.jpg)

Who didn't adore the Cookie Monster growing up? To reminisce in your childhood, you can dress up as a cuter, less hairy version of the Sesame Street character!

## 12. Dorothy

![Dorothy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ro/hr/l186fo3d_600x836.jpg)

If you always loved "The Wizard of Oz", maybe it's time to embody your childhood hero and dress like Dorothy for Halloween!

## 13. Little Red Riding Hood

![Little Red Riding Hood](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sk/h1/ziigbpw1_250x333.jpg)

Little Red Riding Hood is a classic Halloween look for teens! However, where many costumes take a more sexual slant on the costume, this one maintains modesty.

## 14. Snow White

![Snow White](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l9/vh/g13uamnw_528x800.jpg)

Another [traditional costume](https://fashion.allwomenstalk.com/traditional-costumes-from-around-the-world/) is Snow White, but somehow this adorable costume shines above the rest! Better break out your red lipstick and red apples to complete this Halloween ensemble.

## 15. Zombie

![Zombie](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w0/kp/bpphfanu_250x550.jpg)

Lately, zombies have been all the rage! There's certainly no shame in jumping on the bandwagon with this recent craze.

## 16. Huntress

![Huntress](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/af/iq/f27inqod_600x800.jpg)

Whether you feel like Robin Hood or Katniss Everdeen in this costume doesn't matter... you'll look fierce and fabulous either way!

## 17. Pocahontas

![Pocahontas](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vz/m5/s1ub8qy1_600x843.jpg)

There are hundreds of variations on the Pocahontas costume, but this one, complete with fringe and fur boots, is my favorite so far!

## 18. Alice in Wonderland

![Alice in Wonderland](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jt/bs/vbk7ozks_475x641.jpg)

This funky take on Alice in Wonderland will certainly turn heads this Halloween! It’s cute like the **Cheshire cat costume** from #1 but not too much.

## 19. Day of the Dead

![Day of the Dead](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ay/j2/leywp60l_360x539.jpg)

You can get a head start on the Mexican holiday "Dia de los Muertos" or "Day of the Dead" by painting your face like a beautiful skeleton on Halloween.

## 20. Black Cat

![Black Cat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kk/c8/baatnkdd_350x500.jpg)

Black cats are supposed to be bad omens, but there's nothing bad about this purrfect teen costume! If the **Cheshire cat costume** isn’t for you, try a simple black cat costume like this.

## 21. Rock, Paper, Scissors

![Rock, Paper, Scissors](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ht/f5/w97r33qm_400x300.jpg)

If you have two friends willing to do a group costume, why not dress up as rock, paper, and scissors?

## 22. Car Hop Girl

![Car Hop Girl](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ll/je/bot2vrco_500x500.jpg)

I adore this charming, 1950's outfit! If you're vintage at heart, consider this as your costume.

## 23. Crayola Crayon

![Crayola Crayon](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/l4/yt/yo1js474_500x500.jpg)

This costume comes in a variety of colors, so you can dress up as your favorite crayon this year!

## 24. Morton's Salt Girl

![Morton's Salt Girl](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/gi/ub/aeenl1a2_498x494.jpg)

This costume does mean walking around with salt all day, but the outfit is so cute it's definitely worth the unconventional accessory!

## 25. Voodoo Doll

![Voodoo Doll](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sx/mf/d4ynlls4_200x495.jpg)

This costume is frightening, but not too scary, and still cute!

## 26. Sassy Chucky

![Sassy Chucky](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/do/a5/rcx0q4gh_264x400.jpg)

Even scarier is this Chucky costume! But it's more sassy than terrifying, and not creepy enough to make children run away crying when they see you.

## 27. Monster

![Monster](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/m5/vv/eh6oar1g_250x354.jpg)

You're never too old to be a classic, terrifying monster for Halloween! Well, maybe more cute and cuddly than terrifying.

## 28. Tootsie Roll

![Tootsie Roll](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/qt/li/m10y1gx0_300x722.jpg)

Halloween is the only holiday dedicated to candy, so it's only sensible to dress accordingly!

## 29. Flapper

![Flapper](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/li/os/zeliq0zt_400x890.jpg)

This glamorous costume will earn you much praise from your friends when you appear at any Halloween parties wearing it!

Halloween is meant to be a fun time, but sometimes the hunt for the right outfit can be stressful. Did you see any Halloween costumes you want to wear, like perhaps the **cheshire cat costume**? What other ideas for Halloween costumes do you have?
