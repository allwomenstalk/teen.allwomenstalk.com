# 5 Ways to Stand out on the First Day of School ...

The summer holidays are almost over, and it's time to get back to your daily routine! Some might not be excited about going back to school. And we perfectly understand why.

But let’s put that anxiety away. Going back to school does have its perks. If you're wondering how to stand out on the first day of school, keep reading. Shopping for new clothes is one of them. Go shopping, and those first day of school jitters will surely go away. However, you surely don’t want to come off as mediocre on the first day of school. Don’t worry ‘cause we got this covered! We won’t let you go through this alone, so keep scrolling and learn how to stand out on the first day of school.

## 1. PLAN

Deciding what to wear in advance will save you a lot of time getting ready and is one of the best ways to stand out on the first day of school. Planning ahead doesn’t only help you avoid the morning rush. It also gives you more time to visualize your outfits allowing you to figure out which items go together and what doesn't. Decide what impression you’d like to project on the first day. Do you wanna be that sweet gal in a cute dress, or carefree chic in tattered jeans and sneakers? It’s up to you!

## 2. DON'T WEAR EVERYTHING at ONCE

Okay, so you went shopping. And sure enough you are so excited to wear your new clothes all at once. Please, don’t! Chances are, you will be freaking out about having nothing new to wear after a few weeks. That is why it is very important for you to space things out a little bit. Settle for one or two new items every couple of days. Try pairing a new top with your old jeans. Then do the other way around on the next day. This will absolutely save you from the "nothing new to wear" dilemma.

## 3. PLAY down TRENDS

Staying up to date with trends is good, but incorporating them all at once into your school wardrobe isn’t stylish at all. Toning down on trends doesn’t mean that you have to completely abandon them. The secret is to choose one or two key trends, and incorporate them with your everyday school outfit, of course, taking into consideration your school dress code. Updating your outfits with trend-based accessories is always smart and effective.

## 4. CHOOSE COLOR

The thought of going back to school and attending all those classes doesn't always make you feel excited. But wearing something colorful can help enhance your mood and brighten up your day. It’s also a foolproof tactic if you want to be noticed easily.

## 5. BE YOURSELF

Nothing beats confidence when it comes to getting dressed. While we recommend several school style tips, we suggest that you shouldn’t try so hard just to fit in with what everyone else seems to be wearing. Always choose clothes that you are comfortable and confident wearing.

Follow these tips, and you will never be an ordinary school girl!
