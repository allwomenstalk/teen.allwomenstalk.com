# 7 Helpful Websites You Need to Make It through the School Year ...

You should bookmark a few helpful websites for school so that you're prepared when the time comes. You might dread going to class, but these websites can help you learn the topics you need to know, calm you down, and make life easier for you overall. You might as well check them out to see if they fit your needs. So here are some of the most helpful websites for school that will make getting an education more bearable--and maybe even fun:

## 1. Free Rice

This is one of the most helpful websites for school if you need some SAT practice, or just want to expand your knowledge. [Freerice.com](https://freerice.com/) will not only benefit you, but will benefit individuals across the world. The site promises that "for each answer you get right, we donate 10 grains of rice through the World Food Programme to help end hunger." It's the perfect way to help a charity while helping yourself get educated.

## 2. Get Work Done Music

[Getworkdonemusic.com](http://www.getworkdonemusic.com/#) does exactly what the title says. It provides you with motivating music to listen to while you're trying to write an essay or complete a homework assignment. If you don't like the song that's playing, then all you have to do is click a button and a new song will begin. If you try to get work done without headphones in, you might get distracted, so why not try out this website to see if it helps?

## 3. PaperBack Swap

[Paperbackswap.com](https://www.paperbackswap.com/index.php) allows you to swap your old books with someone else. If you need to read The Fault in Our Stars for class this year, you can trade away your old copy of The Great Gatsby book from last semester. It's a great way to help others out, while saving money on new books.

## 4. Sparknotes

[Sparknotes.com](https://www.sparknotes.com/) is known for its ability to help students take the easy way out, but you can use it for other purposes. There are articles about school that will make you laugh, or give you advice to follow. Of course, you can also head to the site to read summaries of a book that you didn't really understand. Just don't use it as an excuse to not read at all.

## 5. Gas Buddy

[Gasbuddy.com](https://www.gasbuddy.com/) is essential if you're driving to school everyday. You don't want to waste more money on gas than you have to. Simply type your area code into this website and it will list the cheapest gas prices around. You might as well save up as much money as you can for those college bills.

## 6. Do Nothing for 2 Minutes

[Donothingfor2minutes.com](http://www.donothingfor2minutes.com/) is useful when you're stressed. You might not realize how important taking a short break can be, but it can alter your entire attitude. When you can't figure out what to write next in your essay, head to the website, listen to the soothing sounds, and let your mind relax for two minutes.

## 7. Essay Typer

_Essaytyper.com_ is helpful when you have parents standing over your shoulder, making sure you do your work. While you need to get it all done eventually, sometimes you need a little break. When you haven't written a word, but want to trick those around you, go to this website and it'll look like you've been hard at work.

School can be stressful, which is why it's nice to have some helpful websites bookmarked. You never know when you'll need to use them. What website do you use every school year?
