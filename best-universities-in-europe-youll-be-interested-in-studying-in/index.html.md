# 7 Best Universities in Europe You'll Be Interested in Studying in ...

Do you want to know which are the best universities in Europe? Well, just read on and discover some of the finest universities you may want to study in. Because the university you choose to study at can have a huge impact on your career, it’s important to consider several factors while selecting a European university such as tuition, location, majors and academics. If you do get in and you have the opportunity to study in one of these amazing universities, try to keep in mind that this thing only will not guarantee your success; your career achievement will depend more on your willingness to work hard and on your determination. Here are 7 of the best universities in Europe you’ll be interested in studying in:

## 1. University of Oxford, United Kingdom

![University of Oxford, United Kingdom](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kv/ox/j8qh64m3_600x370.jpg)

One of the best universities in Europe anyone would be interested in studying in is of course, Oxford University. Opened in 1096, this is the oldest English-speaking university in the world and it offers bachelors, masters and doctorate degrees in Business and Social Sciences, Language and Cultural, Arts and Humanities, Medicine and Health, Engineering and Science and Technology. Oxford has already won Queen’s Anniversary Prizes for Higher Education 9 times, which is more than any other university.

## 2. Universität Hamburg, Germany

![Universität Hamburg, Germany](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jq/fd/np0pq1m2_600x400.jpg)

This university has more than 5000 staff members, almost 38000 student members and it’s one of the largest universities in Germany. It offers a wide range of disciplines you can study, from economics, law and social sciences to the humanities, informatics, medicine and natural sciences.

## 3. Universidad Politécnica De Madrid, Spain

![Universidad Politécnica De Madrid, Spain](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wj/zm/qf5gmv0m_600x399.jpg)

Even though this is an old university and some of its centers are more than 100 hundred years old, it’s still one of the best universities in the world, with around 3000 staff members and 38000 students. It offers bachelors, masters and doctorate degrees in Engineering and Science and Technology, Business and Social Sciences.

## 4. Università Degli Studi Di Bologna, Italy

![Università Degli Studi Di Bologna, Italy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vm/f2/yqd258sn_600x450.jpg)

This is one of the Western world’s oldest universities which serves as a point of reference and a keystone for European culture. It has over 5000 staff members, over 45000 students and it offers 98 degree programs in Arts and Humanities, Language and Cultural, Business and Social Sciences, Medicine and Health, Engineering and Science and Technology.

## 5. The London School of Economics and Political Sciences, United Kingdom

![The London School of Economics and Political Sciences, United Kingdom](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cw/qo/vhb8t12n_600x349.jpg)

This university was founded in 1895 and it specializes in the advanced study of social sciences. Its campus is located in Central London and among the subjects pioneered at this university are Criminology, Social Psychology, Anthropology, International Relations and Sociology. The most amazing thing about this university is the fact that it has already produced more than 35 world leaders and heads of state and 16 Nobel Prize winners in Economics, Peace and Literature.

## 6. Universität Wien, Austria

![Universität Wien, Austria](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/aj/yg/cus5b5y8_600x338.jpg)

This university was founded in 1365, it’s the oldest university in any German-speaking country in the world and it’s the largest research and teaching university in Austria. It has campuses in more than 60 locations; it has over 5000 staff members and 45000 students. There are several restaurants, a library and a few bookshops around the campus.

## 7. University of Cambridge, United Kingdom

![University of Cambridge, United Kingdom](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z2/ht/y60c6f1y_600x400.jpg)

This university was established in 1209 and it’s one of the world’s top-ranking universities. It has over 3000 staff members and around 25000 students. It does bachelors, masters and doctorate degrees in Business and Social Sciences, Language and Cultural, Medicine and Health, Arts and Humanities, Engineering and Science and Technology. Cambridge has already given the world 89 Nobel Prize winners and it has one of the highest levels of graduate employment in the United Kingdom.

There are a lot of amazing universities in Europe that most people would be interested in studying in. I just mentioned a few here but I’m sure that there are many more I could add to this list. What other wonderful universities in Europe do you know? Please tell us about them in the comments section!
