# 9 Best Gift Ideas for Teenage Girls

It can be tough to shop for teenage girls. They are at that in-between stage where they are not quite children anymore, but not quite adults either. They might be into fashion and makeup, or they might prefer more low-key gifts. Whatever their interests, you want to find a gift that is thoughtful and unique. Here are some ideas to get you started.

For the fashionista, consider a gift card to her favorite store. Or, better yet, take her shopping and let her pick out her own clothes. For the beauty lover, a Sephora gift card will let her indulge in all the latest makeup trends. Or, put together a basket of beauty products like nail polish, lip gloss, and body lotion.

If she is into music or movies, look for concert tickets or a new release on DVD or Blu-ray. For the bookworm, an e-reader loaded with books is sure to be a hit. And for the sporty girl, a new pair of running shoes or a yoga mat would be appreciated.

Whatever you choose, make sure it is something that she will enjoy and appreciate. A thoughtful gift will show her how much you care about her and make this holiday season one to remember.

## 1. Music

A good playlist is the perfect gift for any teenage girl. You can put together a mix of her favorite songs, or create a custom playlist with a specific theme in mind.

## 2. Movies

Another great gift idea for teenage girls is a movie night basket. Fill it with her favorite snacks and drinks, along with a few of her favorite films.

## 3. Books

If the teenage girl in your life is a reader, then books are the perfect gift for her. You can either get her a new release that she’s been dying to read, or go for something classic that she’ll love just as much.

## 4. Clothing

Clothing is always a popular gift option for teenage girls. You can either get her something practical that she needs, like a new pair of jeans, or something fun and trendy that she’ll love to show off to her friends.

## 5. Jewelry

Jewelry is another great gift option for teenage girls. Whether you opt for something simple and elegant or something more flashy and statement-making, she’s sure to appreciate the thoughtfulness of this gift.

## 6. Shoes

A new pair of shoes is always a welcome gift for any teenage girl. Whether she’s in need of a new pair of sneakers for running around or some stylish new boots for the winter, she’ll be thrilled to receive them.

## 7. Makeup

If the teenage girl in your life is into makeup, then a makeup set or palette is the perfect gift for her. You can either get her something that she’s been wanting or put together a custom set with her favorite products.

## 8. Tech

For the teenage girl who loves all things tech, a new gadget is the perfect gift. Whether it’s a new phone case or a wireless speaker, she’s sure to appreciate this thoughtful present.

## 9. Experiences

Another great gift idea for teenage girls is to give them an experience they’ll never forget. This could be anything from tickets to their favorite concert to a day at the spa. Whatever you choose, they’re sure to love it!
