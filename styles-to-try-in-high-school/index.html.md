# 7 Trendy High School Styles to Refresh Your Look in 2026

There are endless styles to try in high school, so you shouldn’t ever get bored with the way you dress. High school is a unique time in your life; while it might feel like everyone is watching, it's actually the perfect period to explore your identity. There isn’t anyone you strictly need to impress or any reputation you need to uphold forever, even though the pressure can feel real in the moment. You have 100% freedom to experiment with all of these fun and fashionable styles to try in high school—from the classic **college preppy** look to more edgy aesthetics. So, what are you waiting for? It's time to find your unique high school style and embrace fashion in high school!

## 1. Preppy

![Preppy style high school](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wq/nh/ixj3v6jt_600x893.jpg)

One of the most classic styles to try in high school is the preppy look. It’s clean-cut, adorable, and can easily become your trademark look if you lean into the aesthetic! The preppy look is the perfect cross between a sophisticated business woman and a reserved school girl. If you’re trying to figure out your signature look, I recommend starting with a preppy style as it's versatile and always in season. For more inspiration, check out [Vogue's latest takes](https://www.vogue.com/) on the modern collegiate aesthetic. This style often involves pleated skirts, blazers, and crisp button-downs that give off a polished vibe.

## 2. Elegant

![Elegant high school fashion](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o7/z2/esbw5gut_600x995.jpg)

People hardly ever expect to see high schoolers dressed elegantly, and that’s exactly why you should dress that way! Standing out with a refined look shows confidence, and honestly, feeling pretty is an amazing feeling that can boost your mood all day. For inspiration for elegant outfits, I often look at classic Lolita looks, which are sweet, feminine, and slightly dramatic. You can also incorporate [modern fashion trends](https://fashion.allwomenstalk.com/modern-fashion-trends/) like silk slips or tailored trousers to keep the elegance feeling fresh and contemporary rather than dated.

## 3. California Casual

![California Casual look](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/y7/p2/sh43e3t1_426x640.jpg)

On the opposite end of the spectrum is the California casual look. It’s important to try out looks like this too, however, because you can’t be preppy and elegant all of the time. Sometimes you just want to feel relaxed while still looking put-together. The California casual look is just as stylish as the others, in my opinion, but this laid-back look may be more your style if you prefer comfort over stiff fabrics. Think light denim, breezy tees, and easy-to-wear sneakers.

| Style | Key Pieces | Best Occasion |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Preppy | Blazers, Pleated Skirts | Presentations |
| Elegant | Silk Blouses, Tailored Pants | School Dances |
| California Casual | Denim Shorts, Graphic Tees | Friday Classes |

## 4. Sophisticated

![Sophisticated high school look](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/pv/sp/fkk6un37_423x635.jpg)

This look isn’t for everyone, but I know several people who absolutely rock it! You don’t have to be a 40-year-old executive to dress in sophisticated attire. Teens should try out a sophisticated look in high school; you’ll often find you earn more respect when you’re out and about the town or even during important school events. Next time you're at a loss for what to wear, reach for some neutral tones and structured pieces that echo the [elegant outfits](#2) we discussed earlier. [Refinery29 often features](https://www.refinery29.com/en-us/fashion) great ways to make sophisticated pieces feel youth-appropriate and trendy.

## 5. High Fashion

![High Fashion high school](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ho/m0/w3jjcamq_500x664.jpg)

I don’t know if I would ever be so bold as to go this route, but I have faith that you could make it work! High fashion is edgy, spunky, and always changing! If that mirrors your personality at all, I’d recommend trying out a high fashion look at school to really showcase your creativity. Experimenting with avant-garde pieces or bold textures can help you get a pretty solid feel of what style works best for you. This is all about taking risks and wearing what makes you feel like you're on a runway.

Must-have High Fashion accessories to elevate your high school look:

- Chunky statement jewelry

- Oversized sunglasses

- Designer-inspired belts

- Bold, patterned scarves

## 6. Vintage

![Vintage high school style](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sm/so/lyv45ept_600x831.jpg)

Vintage looks have made a huge comeback and for good reason! Whether you're sourcing from thrift stores or your grandma's closet, these pieces offer a unique flair that fast fashion simply can't replicate. While some may say vintage has become "mainstream," you can still make it your own by mixing eras. Vintage outfits are undeniably classy and adorable, helping you stand out in a crowd and become a diamond in the rough at your school. To make this work, focus on [building a capsule wardrobe](https://fashion.allwomenstalk.com/building-a-capsule-wardrobe/) of timeless vintage staples like high-waisted jeans or '90s-style floral dresses.

## 7. Clueless

![Clueless style Cher Horowitz](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/eo/lo/hygzk45c_600x902.jpg)

I truly don’t believe an article on teen style could be complete without a reference to the film which epitomized '90s teen fashion, _Clueless_. One of my favorite movies, _Clueless_ taught me endless lessons about fashion and helped define my own sense of style. The yellow plaid suit is iconic, but the movie offers so many more subtle looks too. For specific tips about how to dress like the greatest fashion mastermind of all, Cher Horowitz, check out my article on Clueless fashion here: [7 Fashion Musts to Achieve Cher's Style from Clueless](https://fashion.allwomenstalk.com/fashion-musts-to-achieve-chers-style-from-clueless/). This aesthetic is perfect for anyone who wants a blend of [preppy](#1) and playful!

I love mixing up my style! So I try to do so as frequently as possible, and I encourage you to do the same.
